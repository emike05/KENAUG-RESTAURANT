Menu

The Chef's Special

Grilled Chicken.
$50

One of the greatest advantages of our grilled chicken is its simplicity. With just a few basic ingredients and some heat, you can transform a plain chicken breast into a savory masterpiece. The process of grilling adds a unique smoky flavor and creates a beautiful charred exterior, enhancing the overall taste and texture of the meat. .

Bowl of Soup
$20

When the weather turns chilly or you simply crave a comforting meal, there's nothing quite like a steaming bowl of soup to soothe the soul from us and warm you from the inside out. From classic favorites to exotic creations, a bowl of soup offers a world of flavors, textures, and nourishment. .

Fresh Hot Bread
$15

Few things can rival the sheer delight of biting into a slice of freshly baked, hot bread. The enticing aroma, the soft texture, and the warmth of our bread that emanates from each bite create a truly blissful experience. Whether it's a loaf of crusty artisan bread, a fluffy dinner roll, or a warm baguette, hot bread straight from the oven is a treat that never fails to satisfy..

Pizza
$45

The beauty of Kenaug pizza lies in its versatility. Whether you prefer a crispy thin crust or a pillowy thick crust, there's a pizza style to suit every craving. From the traditional Neapolitan pizza, with its soft and chewy crust, to the New York-style pizza, known for its large, foldable slices, each style offers a unique eating experience.

Burger.
$15

At its core, a kenaug burger consists of a ground meat patty, typically beef, that is seasoned and cooked to perfection. The patty is often grilled, pan-fried, or charbroiled, creating a flavorful crust on the outside while retaining its succulent juiciness inside. However, burgers have evolved to include various meat alternatives such as turkey, chicken, pork, or even vegetarian and vegan options, catering to a diverse range of dietary preferences.

Curry Sauce.
$14

Curry sauce, with its vibrant colors and aromatic blend of spices, is a culinary delight that has captivated taste buds around the world. Originating from the Indian subcontinent but adopted and adapted by many cultures, curry sauce offers a rich and complex flavor profile that tantalizes the senses..
